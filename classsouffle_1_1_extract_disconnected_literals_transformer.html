Detailed Description

Transformation pass to move literals out of a clause and into new relations if they are independent of head arguments.

E.g. a(x) :- b(x), c(y), d(y). is transformed into:

  • a(x) :- b(x), newrel().
  • newrel() :- c(y), d(y).
